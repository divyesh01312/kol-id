How do we calculate the engagement rate?

Engagement on YouTube encompasses every interaction from your audience‚Äîlikes, comments, and even shares. The formula to calculate engagement rate is straightforward: Add up all the engagements (likes, comments, etc.) across their posts, divide by their total subscribers, and multiply by 100.‚ÄçIt reveals the median engagement rate for posts, so we tend to exclude statistical outliers for a fair assessment. We base our calculations on the most recent 30 influencer posts, ensuring you have the latest and most accurate engagement data.

What is the ideal video length for high engagement on YouTube?

Up to 2 minutes, engagement stays strong. But from 2 to 6 minutes, you might see a drop. Beyond 6 minutes, if you hold on until 12 minutes, viewers tend to stick around. However, after the 12-minute mark, expect a gradual decline. Keep this in mind: videos over 12 minutes are best only for narratives and in-depth tutorials. Understanding this balance can be a game-changer for your influencer strategy on YouTube.
